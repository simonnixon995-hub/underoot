Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

We want you to be happy with your order. This policy sets out your rights under the UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 and the Consumer Rights Act 2015, and how to return an item.

Your 14-day right to cancel

You can cancel your order within 14 days of receiving it, for any reason. To cancel, email undroot0@gmail.com with your order number. You then have 14 days to send the item back.

Condition of returns (hygiene)

Because our products are cosmetics applied to the scalp and hair, items can only be returned if they are unused and still sealed. For health and hygiene reasons, we cannot accept the return of a product once its seal has been opened, unless the item is faulty. This is permitted under the Consumer Contracts Regulations for sealed goods not suitable for return once unsealed.

Refunds

Once we receive your returned item (or proof you've sent it), we'll refund you within 14 days, to your original payment method. Standard refunds include the original delivery cost; if you chose a faster delivery upgrade, we refund the standard rate.

Return postage

For a change-of-mind cancellation, you pay the cost of returning the item. We recommend a tracked service, as you're responsible for the item until it reaches us.

Faulty, damaged or wrong items

If your item arrives faulty, damaged or incorrect, contact us at undroot0@gmail.com within a reasonable time with a photo, and we'll arrange a replacement or full refund including any return postage. This does not affect your statutory rights.

How to return

Email undroot0@gmail.com first — we'll confirm the return address and next steps before you send anything.
