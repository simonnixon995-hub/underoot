Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This policy explains how Underoot ("we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit our website or place an order. We comply with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Underoot is a UK sole-trader business. Address: 21B Pydar Street, Truro, TR1 2AY, United Kingdom. For any privacy question, contact us at undroot0@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Information you give us: your name, delivery address, email and order details when you buy. Card details are entered on Stripe's secure checkout — we never see or store your card number.
  • Information collected automatically: basic usage data and cookies, including the TikTok pixel, to understand how visitors use the site and to measure marketing.

How we use it

  • To process, fulfil and deliver your order.
  • To contact you about your order and provide customer support.
  • To measure and improve our website and marketing.
  • To send marketing only where you have agreed; you can opt out at any time.

Who we share it with

We share only what's needed to run the shop, with:

  • Stripe — to process payments securely.
  • Our fulfilment partner — to produce and ship your order, which may involve transferring your delivery details to the EU.
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security.
  • TikTok — advertising and analytics via the pixel.
  • Couriers — to deliver your parcel.

We do not sell your personal data.

International transfers

Because our product is fulfilled from the EU, your delivery details may be transferred outside the UK. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards recognised under UK data protection law.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email undroot0@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Retention

We keep order and tax records for as long as the law requires, and other personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above.

Changes

We may update this policy; the latest version will always appear on this page.
