About Underoot

Scalp-first hair care, made simple.

Underoot started from a simple idea: healthier-looking hair starts at the scalp — the one layer most oils ignore. Instead of another heavy oil that just coats the strand, we focus on feeding the scalp.

What we make

Our Rosemary Hair & Scalp Strengthening Oil is a lightweight, fast-absorbing blend of nine plant oils led by rosemary — COSMOS Organic certified, vegan and cruelty-free. It supports the look and condition of your hair and scalp. It is a cosmetic, not a medicine or a hair-loss treatment.

How it works

One product, two minutes a day. No drugs, no synthetic actives, no twelve-step routine — just a considered blend applied to the scalp.

Who we are

Underoot is a UK sole-trader business based in Truro. Every order is made to order and sent with free tracked UK shipping. We're a small operation and we read every message — say hello at undroot0@gmail.com.
