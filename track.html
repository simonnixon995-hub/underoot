Where's my order?

When you place an order you'll get a confirmation email from our secure checkout straight away. As soon as your order is packed and dispatched, we'll email you a tracking link so you can follow it to your door.

Haven't had your tracking yet?

Every order is made to order, so there's a short window before it ships. If it's been a few days, please check your spam folder first — confirmation and tracking emails sometimes land there.

Need a hand?

Email us with the name and email address you ordered with and we'll find your order and resend your tracking right away: undroot0@gmail.com.
