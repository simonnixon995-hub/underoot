# [BRAND] — Landing Page Image Prompts

## Global style suffix
Append to every prompt below for a cohesive look:

> `premium natural haircare brand aesthetic, soft diffused natural daylight, minimal clean composition, earthy botanical palette of sage green / warm amber / cream, shallow depth of field, editorial apothecary mood, photorealistic, ultra-detailed, no text, no logos, no watermark`

Generate everything at the aspect ratio each slot needs (hero wide/portrait for mobile, squares for ingredient/texture, etc.).

## 1. Hero background / brand mood  → Section 1
A single sprig of fresh rosemary resting beside three scattered drops of golden hair oil on a smooth cream stone surface, warm morning light raking in from the left casting soft long shadows, a quiet sense of ritual and calm, generous empty space on the right for headline text. + *style suffix*

## 2. Oil texture macro  → Sections 5–6
Extreme macro close-up of a glass dropper releasing a single droplet of clear golden botanical oil, the drop catching warm backlight, suspended mid-fall above a glossy pool of oil with subtle concentric ripples, deep amber highlights. + *style suffix*

## 3. Ingredient flat-lay  → Section 6
Overhead flat-lay of raw haircare botanicals on a cream linen surface with deliberate negative space: fresh rosemary sprigs, glossy castor beans, pale pumpkin seeds, dried lavender buds, and a small ceramic dish of golden oil, arranged loosely and naturally. + *style suffix*

## 4. Scalp-first concept  → Section 4 / 7
Clean minimalist editorial illustration contrasting two ideas side by side: on the left a single isolated hair strand, on the right a soft cross-section showing a healthy scalp surface with one hair root beneath it, delicate botanical line-art, sage and amber tones, abundant white space, calm and clinical-but-warm. + *style suffix*
*(Alternative: build this as a simple flat vector graphic in design software — often cleaner than AI for a diagram.)*

## 5. Application ritual  → Section 8 (shows the method, NOT a result)
Close-up shot from above of a hand parting hair to apply a few drops of oil with a glass dropper directly to the scalp, focus on the dropper and the parting line, soft warm bathroom light, calm self-care mood, face not shown, hair already healthy and full. + *style suffix*

## 6. Lifestyle shelf  → backdrop for compositing the REAL bottle
A minimal bathroom shelf scene with clear empty central space reserved for a product, cream zellige tiles, a small trailing plant, a folded linen towel, soft natural window light, spa-like calm. + *style suffix*
*(Composite your real Selfnamed product photo into the empty centre — do not generate the bottle.)*

## 7. Section divider texture  → between sections
Soft abstract background of blurred golden oil droplets and water bubbles with a faint sage-green tint, gentle gradient, dreamy and out of focus, suitable as a subtle full-width divider. + *style suffix*

---

## DO NOT GENERATE — source these real

- **Product shots (hero bottle, offer image):** use Selfnamed's actual product photography of the bottle they ship, or composite that real bottle into scene #6. A generated bottle that doesn't match what arrives = returns and broken trust.
- **Results / before-after (Section 9):** real customer photos only. Never generate or fake a transformation. At launch you have none — replace the results section with the ritual/how-it-works imagery (#5) until real ones exist. When you have them: same person, same lighting, true timeline, "individual results may vary."
- **Reviews (Section 10):** real screenshots/quotes only.
- **No identifiable real people** in any generated image.
