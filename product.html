Description
A nutrient-rich oil serum infused with rosemary and peppermint to nourish the scalp, improve hair resilience and restore shine to dry or brittle hair. A lightweight blend of nine plant oils led by rosemary — it sinks in like a serum, never greasy. A cosmetic hair-enhancing oil; not a medicine or hair-loss treatment.
How to use
  • 1. Use once daily, morning or night. Shake well before use.
  • 2. Use the dropper to apply directly to a dry or towel-dried scalp, wherever you'd like.
  • 3. Massage into the scalp and let it soak in — a slight tingling is normal. Leave in, or rinse after an hour.
